Save the Amazon Forest

STAF is a foundation that is involved with conserving the Amazon rainforest which is the largest tropical rainforest in the world. It consists of the largest collection of living plants and animal species in the world. STAF is working with flutterwave and Startng to help raise awareness about conserving nature and its habitants.
